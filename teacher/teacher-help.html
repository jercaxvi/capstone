Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.
How do I take attendance?
Navigate to your class page and click on the "Attendance" tab. Select the date and mark students as present, absent, or late. Don't forget to save your changes.
How can I send messages to parents?
Not available right now.
How do I submit grades?
Access your class, go to the "Grades" tab, select the grading period, enter scores for each student, and click "Submit". Make sure to double-check all entries before final submission.
